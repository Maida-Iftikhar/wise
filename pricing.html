    Frequently asked
    Questions

    Do I lose access to the licenses if I remove a teacher?

    No, you retain the licenses and they remain unused until someone is given access to them.

    Suppose I have 50 licenses for my institute, how many teachers can I add to my institute?

    You can add as many teachers as required, there is no upper limit. Your teachers will be able to take up to 50 concurrent sessions simultaneously.

    Do I lose the data and analytics for the teachers who have been deactivated?

    No, the analytics of the classrooms and the sessions taken by these teachers are available to you even after the deactivation of the teacher’s license

    If I buy before the expiry of my trial period, would the plan start after the expiry of the trial?

    Yes, the plan will start once your trial period is over

    We already have a few zoom licenses. Is there any way we could use those licenses on Wise?

    Yes, there is a way. Please get in touch with Sales.
